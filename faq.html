Frequently Asked Questions




What types of flowers are suitable for different occasions?

Roses are a classic choice for romantic occasions, while lilies and daisies are great for expressing sympathy. Orchids and tulips are versatile and work well for various celebrations.

How do I care for freshly cut flowers to make them last longer?

Trim the stems at an angle, change the water every few days, and keep them in a cool location away from direct sunlight. Adding flower food to the water can also extend their freshness.

Can I customize a bouquet for a special occasion?

Absolutely! We offer personalized bouquets tailored to your preferences and the occasion. Contact our team, and we'll be happy to create a unique arrangement for you.

What flowers are in season during a specific time of the year?

Seasonal flowers vary, but for example, peonies and sunflowers are common in the summer, while tulips and daffodils bloom in the spring. Contact us for specific seasonal recommendations.

Can I include a personalized message with my flower delivery?

Absolutely! When placing your order, you can include a personalized message that will be attached to the bouquet. It's a great way to add a special touch to your gift.

What is the best way to choose flowers for a wedding?

Consider the wedding theme, color scheme, and personal preferences. Our florists are here to help you choose the perfect flowers that complement the overall style of your wedding.

Do you offer flower subscriptions or recurring delivery services?

Yes, we provide flower subscription services. You can choose the frequency of delivery, and each time, you'll receive a fresh and beautifully curated bouquet right to your doorstep.

Do you provide flower delivery services, and how much does it cost?

Yes, we offer flower delivery. The cost depends on the delivery location and the size of the arrangement. You can find more details on our website or contact us for a personalized quote.
